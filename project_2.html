Netflix Originals Data Analysis

Keywords: web-scraping, data visualization, Selenium, Matplotlib

Objective

The objective of this study is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of Netflix Original productions, leveraging data scraped from IMDb and focusing on their proliferation, audience reception, and thematic diversity from 2012 to 2023. The aim is to identify patterns in production volume, genre popularity, and quality as perceived by viewers, to uncover the types of content that resonate with audiences and how the platform's strategies align with viewer demand.
